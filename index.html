  • Market(r)

    TurboTax for Marketing

    Co-Founder

    When growth-stage businesses look for marketing (product development, advertising, positioning, branding, etc), they typically do 1 of 3 things:
    1) Build it in house from day one. They pool so many resources insourcing that they then don’t have the gas to put into the car, so to speak.
    2) They hire their friend that “does marketing.”
    3) They hire an agency. Agencies are kind of like Tylenol. Tylenol is great for its particular use case, but not for all use cases. And you don’t want to take too much Tylenol.

    Market(r) uses data science to give the business a list of yes/no recommendations. In the matter of seconds, the business is diagnosed, a prescription is given, and we are monitoring results.

  • Influenc(r)

    Micro Influencer Discovery

    Co-Founder

    WHAT IS THE MOST EFFECTIVE WAY TO SELL SOMETHING?
    A referral from a friend.
    BUT HOW DO YOU SCALE THAT?
    You can't possibly ask everybody for a referral.
    ENTER: INFLUENCERS.
    The struggle that every brand has is getting the trust of their demographic. This is why referrals work. It bypasses the trust layer in the customer journey.
    INFLUENCER MARKETING CREATES REFERRALS FROM FRIENDS AT SCALE.
    Influencers ≠ Celebrities. Celebrities are celebrated. Influencers are influential. True influencers create a connection with each and every follower. You may not recognize them at a coffee shop, but they influence purchase behavior more than any comparable form of marketing.
